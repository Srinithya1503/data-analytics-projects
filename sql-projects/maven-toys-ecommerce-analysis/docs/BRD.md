# 📄 **BUSINESS REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT (BRD)**

### 1. **Situation**

The company operates a boutique DTC e-commerce business with four core products. Leadership lacks clarity on true profitability, refund risk, and marketing efficiency.

### 2. **Task**

Create a financially accurate, analysis-ready data layer to:
Audit profitability
Identify growth risks
Guide marketing investment decisions

### 3. **Business Questions**

Which products generate the most net profit?
How do refunds impact margins?
Can the business scale without repeat purchases?
What is the maximum CAC per product?

### 4. **KPIs Defined**

Net Revenue
Net Profit
Profit Margin %
Refund Ratio %
Breakeven CAC

### 5. **Assumptions**

Refund rate simulated at 5% (industry-realistic)
Refunds never exceed item price
All revenue is order-item driven

### 6. **Risks Identified**

Over-reliance on hero products
No customer retention loop
Marketing overspend without CAC clarity

### 7. Recommendations

Anchor all promotions around Product 1
Introduce premium tier SKU to raise AOV
Set CAC caps per product based on breakeven analysis
Avoid retention-heavy investments
Focus analytics on order-level optimization
