***Business Interpretation***

###### ***Recommendation***



1. Design bundled promotions anchored on Product 1
2. Use Product 4 as the primary attach product
3. De-prioritize Product 2 \& 3 bundles unless anchored to Product 1





###### 2️⃣ ***Customer Purchase Depth***



###### ***Observed Behavior***



1. Customers typically buy 2–4 products
2. Purchase happens within 1–3 orders
3. Very few customers exceed 3 orders



###### ***Insight***



1. This is not a replenishment or loyalty-driven model.
2. It is a single-occasion, high-intent gift business.



###### ***Recommendation***



1. Focus analytics on order-level optimization
2. Avoid retention-heavy strategies (subscriptions, points programs)
